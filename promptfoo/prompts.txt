- Tag as "investment" if the news:
  - Directly relates to funding rounds (e.g., seed, Series A, B, etc.)
  - Is about strategic investments by firms into other companies or projects
  - Announces new investment vehicles or funds

- Tag as "fund" if the news:
  - Relates to the launch of a new investment fund
  - Is about significant fundraising milestones for a fund
  - Discusses major changes in a fund's investment strategy
  - Is about withdrawal or liquidation of a fund
  - Reports on notable performance metrics of a fund
  - Is about the allocation of funds to specific sectors or projects

Do not tag as "investment" or "fund" if the news:
  - Is about regulatory updates not directly affecting investment vehicles or funds
